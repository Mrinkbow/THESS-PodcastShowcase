Timeline

  • November 2023 (Start of THESS) THESS was created as a school project for the subjects Math, Social Studies, and TLE/LifeLabs (an APEC subject). We thoroughly planned many different aspects of our channel through a worksheet. Once it was approved, we created the channel.
  • Feb 2024 (First Video Creation) We had planned on the channel being educational and focused on Social Studies, so decided to have Martial Law as the topic of our first video. But a video just discussing would lack entertainment, so we had the idea of baking while discussing. After a very long editing phase, we uploaded the video on February 26, 2024.
  • April 2024 (Second Video Creation) PAfter analysing the first video, we had to create a plan for our second video. We landed on the topic of the Bubonic Plague, and instead of baking, we decided to cook. Our first video had various errors we had to learn from, so our second video is quite the improvement
